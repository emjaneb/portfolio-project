About Emma

Hi I’m Emma, a passionate junior front-end web developer from Melbourne, Australia. With a background originally in retail, I have recently started an exciting journey into the world of web development. My career transition was fuelled by a desire for creativity and a thirst for new challenges.

As I started on a new career path, I enrolled in online workshops with SheCodes, which has been an invaluable experience that introduced me to the exciting world of web development. These workshops have empowered me with a solid foundation in front-end web development and ignited a creative spark that drives my commitment to this exciting field.

Beyond coding, my life is coloured with various interests. I'm an avid reader, music also has a special place in my heart; it fuels my creativity and offers the perfect backdrop for those long coding sessions. Additionally, I enjoy my time spent outdoors, whether it’s in country Victoria where I currently live, or at the beach back near my hometown.

As I continue my web development journey, I aim to grow and refine my skills, expand my knowledge, and collaborate with talented professionals in the field. If you'd like to get in touch or discuss potential projects, feel free to reach out.
